About Us

     True North TTC is a welcoming community for players of all ages and skills. Whether you're just getting started or you are an experienced player, we have a coach to pair you up with and countless playing partners for you to practice with. We hope to spread the joy of the sport and help as many people as possible to discover table tennis. If you have any questions about anything about the club or table tennis itself, feel free to reach out to us! Our contact information is on the contact us page and we hope to see you here in the future!
